=== SECTION: INVOCATIONAL_GLOSS ===

POPULATION_DENSITY_GLOSS:
- SPARSE: low-density settlement pattern
- MODERATE: ordinary mixed-density settlement pattern
- HIGH: dense urban or clustered population pattern
- EXTREME: hyper-dense settlement pattern

POPULATION_DIVERSITY_GLOSS:
- HOMOGENOUS: low cultural or demographic variation
- MIXED: moderate visible variation
- COSMOPOLITAN: high pluralism and strong cross-group contact

TECHNOLOGICAL_LEVEL_GLOSS:
- PRE_INDUSTRIAL: pre-mechanized or early agrarian craft world
- INDUSTRIAL: machine-era industrial society
- INFORMATION_AGE: networked digital society
- NEAR_FUTURE: slightly advanced extrapolation from present-day systems
- BIOPUNK: biotechnology-saturated or bio-modified society
- POST_SCARCITY: material scarcity substantially reduced
- OTHER: setting-specific technological condition outside the standard ladder

DATA_ACCESS_AND_PRIVACY_GLOSS:
- OPEN_PUBLIC_NETWORK: open information ecology with wide general access
- CORPORATE_OWNED: information infrastructure primarily controlled by firms
- STATE_MONITORED: information ecology strongly supervised by public authority
- DATA_HAVENS: fragmented or sanctuary-based privacy structures

WORLDVIEW_GLOSS:
- These fields are invocation-rich normative priors that shape interpretation, trust, authority response, and long-horizon expectation.
- Enum literals above are canonical write-surface values.
- Explanatory meanings live here and are not separate mutable state fields.
