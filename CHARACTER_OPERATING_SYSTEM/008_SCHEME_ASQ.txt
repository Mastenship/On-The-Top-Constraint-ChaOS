=== SECTION: ATTACHMENT_MARKERS ===

ATTACHMENT_STYLE: ENUM [SECURE, ANXIOUS_PREOCCUPIED, AVOIDANT_DISMISSIVE, FEARFUL_AVOIDANT]
ATTACHMENT_SECURITY: QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE

QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE: ENUM [VERY_LOW, LOW, AVERAGE, HIGH, VERY_HIGH]

QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE_GLOSS:
- VERY_LOW: below 20th percentile
- LOW: 20th–40th percentile
- AVERAGE: 40th–60th percentile
- HIGH: 60th–80th percentile
- VERY_HIGH: above 80th percentile

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: STYLE_DEFINITIONS ===

SECURE: Comfortable with intimacy and autonomy.
ANXIOUS_PREOCCUPIED: High intimacy need, abandonment fear.
AVOIDANT_DISMISSIVE: Independence over intimacy, emotion suppression.
FEARFUL_AVOIDANT: Desire intimacy but fear hurt.

- Style highly stable.
- Security percentile biases attachment impulses.
- Shifts require prolonged corrective experiences or explicit instantiation-time override.
