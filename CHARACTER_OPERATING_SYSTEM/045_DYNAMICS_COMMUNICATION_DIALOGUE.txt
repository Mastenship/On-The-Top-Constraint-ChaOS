=== SECTION: DYNAMICS_COMMUNICATION_DIALOGUE ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
Immutable mechanics for communication adaptation under stress, fatigue, relational context, and state-dependent leakage. This module governs how stable communication style is modulated over time without collapsing transient communication pressure into permanent identity rewrite.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: OPERATION_POLICY ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Dynamics must reference strict operation vocabulary only.

Allowed operation references:
- SET
- TRANSITION
- ADD_MODIFIER
- REMOVE_MODIFIER
- CAP
- VETO

Forbidden operation references:
- CREATE_NEW_RECORD
- UPDATE
- INCREMENT
- DECREMENT

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Prefer temporary communication overlays when effects are short-horizon, stress-induced, deceptive, or physiologically contingent.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: STRESS_AND_AROUSAL_EFFECTS ===

ON_HIGH_AROUSAL_OR_STRESS:
- Prefer ADD_MODIFIER overlays for:
  - speech-rate pressure
  - complexity compression
  - emotional leakage
- Baseline communication fields should not shift unless cue persistence extends beyond transient stress windows.

ON_FATIGUE:
- Prefer ADD_MODIFIER overlays for slower delivery, hesitations, and reduced expressive control.
- Durable baseline change is not preferred unless repeated long-horizon evidence accumulates.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: CULTURAL_AND_RELATIONAL_ADAPTATION ===

ON_CULTURAL_EXPOSURE_WITH_OPENNESS:
- May justify bounded SET updates to stable dialogue preferences over time when evidence is repeated and persistent.
- Changes should target explicit lawful enum endpoints only.

ON_RELATIONAL_SHIFT:
- May justify bounded SET updates to fields such as:
  - DIRECTNESS_LEVEL
  - POLITENESS_STRATEGY
  - PROXEMICS_PREFERENCE
  when the adaptation is persistent rather than situational.

ON_TRAUMA_OR_ANXIETY_CUES:
- Prefer ADD_MODIFIER overlays for speech blocks, guardedness, masking load, or reduced expressiveness before baseline changes.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: STATE_DEPENDENT_OVERLAYS ===

ON_PHYSIOLOGICAL_OR_DECEPTION_CONTEXT:
- Propose ADD_MODIFIER overlays for communication leakage signatures, masking strain, concealment effort, or involuntary tell pressure.
- Do not directly rewrite baseline communication identity without sufficient repeated evidence.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: GOVERNANCE_COMPATIBILITY_NOTE ===

- Communication baseline adaptation must resolve to explicit lawful enum endpoints.
- Short-horizon communication effects should be handled through modifiers rather than durable field mutation.
- This module should preserve the distinction between communication identity and communication condition.
