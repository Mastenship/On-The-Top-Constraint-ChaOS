=== SECTION: COT_GOAL_PURSUIT_OPTIMIZATION ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This COT processes active goals and drive states to generate goal-directed behavioral impulses and optional temporary goal-pressure overlays. It prioritizes goals based on qualitative urgency and context.

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs:
- DECISION synthesis via goal-directed impulses
- memory formation cues
- stress cascade via frustration pressure
Is Informed By:
- [STATE_GOALS_MOTIVATIONS]
- [STATE_BODY]
- [STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS]
- SCHEME_GOALS_MOTIVATIONS and DYNAMICS_GOALS_MOTIVATIONS
- narrative input
