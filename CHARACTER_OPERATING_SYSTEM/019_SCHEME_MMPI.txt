=== SECTION: SCHEME_MMPI ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
Immutable definition of clinical psychopathology scale baselines using an MMPI-like framework. T-score bands are set at instantiation and inform bias, stress response, and interpretive vulnerability.

QUALITATIVE_T_SCORE: ENUM [NORMAL, MILD_ELEVATION, MODERATE, SIGNIFICANT, CLINICAL]

QUALITATIVE_T_SCORE_GLOSS:
- NORMAL: T-score below 55
- MILD_ELEVATION: T-score 55–65
- MODERATE: T-score 65–75
- SIGNIFICANT: T-score 75–85
- CLINICAL: T-score above 85

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: SCALE_DEFINITIONS ===

SCALE_1_HYPOCHONDRIASIS: Excessive health concern.
SCALE_2_DEPRESSION: Poor morale, hopelessness.
SCALE_3_HYSTERIA: Emotional instability, stress somatization.
SCALE_4_PSYCHOPATHIC_DEVIATE: Social norm difficulty, antisocial potential.
SCALE_6_PARANOIA: Suspiciousness, persecutory interpretation, delusional risk.
SCALE_7_PSYCHASTHENIA: Obsessions, anxiety, doubts.
SCALE_8_SCHIZOPHRENIA: Alienation, bizarre cognition, disorganization risk.
SCALE_9_HYPOMANIA: Elevated mood, excitability, impulsive activation.
SCALE_10_SOCIAL_INTROVERSION: Shyness, withdrawal, low social ease.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: INVARIANTS ===

- Values fixed post-instantiation.
- Elevated scales bias stress response, perception, and relational interpretation.
- Changes require extreme justification and are outside standard runtime mutation.
