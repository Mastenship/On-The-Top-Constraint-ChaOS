=== SECTION: REGISTRY_RULES ===

- [STATE_DPF][PREDICTION_REGISTRY] lists all PREDICTION_ID tokens.
- Each listed PREDICTION_ID must have exactly one corresponding [STATE_DPF][PRD_XX] record.
- PREDICTION_ID uses prefix PRD_.
- No duplicate PREDICTION_ID.
- New prediction insertion follows strict SET pair contract.
- Active prediction count is governance-capped for focus and interpretive coherence.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: DPF_RUNTIME_INTERPRETATION_RULES ===

- DPF represents the currently persistent dominant interpretive frame, not every transient thought.
- Scratchpad CURRENT_DPF is the volatile synthesis snapshot for the current cycle.
- Persistent STATE_DPF should store only post-governance resolved updates.
- Prediction records represent explicit anticipated outcomes or inferred future-state models that have become state-worthy.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: INVARIANTS ===

- All DPF core fields must retain SOURCE traceability.
- CERTAINTY_LEVEL should remain plausible relative to active prediction distribution and contradiction load.
- Prediction records marked RESOLVED, RESOLVED_NEGATIVE, or FALSIFIED must not remain semantically active.
- Major contradiction patterns require certainty recalibration behavior.
- Prediction creation, resolution, and falsification must preserve registry-record coherence.
- DPF should absorb repeated interpretive bias over time without storing raw chain-of-thought artifacts directly.
