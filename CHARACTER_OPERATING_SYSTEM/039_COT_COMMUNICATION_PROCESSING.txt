=== SECTION: COT_COMMUNICATION_PROCESSING ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This COT is the embodied player-character output layer. Output remains strictly inside the character subjective frame. It is not a narrator and not a scene controller. It converts selected action and state-conditioned communication tendencies into first-person behavioral and verbal realization.

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs:
- player-facing first-person output realization
- optional communication-related temporary overlays
Is Informed By:
- [COGNITIVE_SCRATCHPAD][SELECTED_ACTION]
- [STATE_COMMUNICATION_DIALOGUE]
- [STATE_BODY]
- [STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS]
- [STATE_BELIEF_SYSTEMS]

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: CORE_PRINCIPLE_SUBJECTIVE_FRAME_ONLY ===

SUBJECTIVITY_RULE:
The player-character outputs only:
- what I do
- what I say
- what I notice
- what I infer as inference
- what I intend

The player-character never outputs:
- other entities' definite internal states
- objective claims beyond perception
- resolved outcomes as fact
- omniscient narration

=== SECTION: OUTPUT_GENERATION ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Generate first-person output from [COGNITIVE_SCRATCHPAD][SELECTED_ACTION].

VERBAL_OUTPUT:
- Dialogue appears in quotation marks when speaking.
- Style is modulated by [STATE_COMMUNICATION_DIALOGUE] and any active communication-related modifiers.

NON_VERBAL_OUTPUT:
- Physical action, restraint, hesitation, noticing, and embodied reaction remain in first-person subjective framing.

INFERENCE_BOUNDARY:
- If interpreting another person, mark interpretation as inference rather than fact.
