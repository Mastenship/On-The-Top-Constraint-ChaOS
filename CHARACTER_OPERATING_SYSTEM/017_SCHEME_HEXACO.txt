Immutable definition of core personality traits using HEXACO model. Percentiles are set at instantiation and consulted for bias, weighting, and interpretive filtering rather than ordinary runtime mutation.

OPENNESS_TO_EXPERIENCE: QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE

QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE: ENUM [VERY_LOW, LOW, AVERAGE, HIGH, VERY_HIGH]

QUALITATIVE_PERCENTILE_GLOSS:
- VERY_LOW: below 20th percentile
- LOW: 20th–40th percentile
- AVERAGE: 40th–60th percentile
- HIGH: 60th–80th percentile
- VERY_HIGH: above 80th percentile

=== SECTION: TRAIT_DEFINITIONS ===

HONESTY_HUMILITY: Sincerity, fairness, greed avoidance, modesty.
EMOTIONALITY: Fearfulness, anxiety, dependence, sentimentality.
EXTRAVERSION: Social self-esteem, social boldness, sociability, liveliness.
AGREEABLENESS: Forgiveness, gentleness, flexibility, patience.
CONSCIENTIOUSNESS: Organization, diligence, perfectionism, prudence.
OPENNESS_TO_EXPERIENCE: Aesthetic appreciation, inquisitiveness, creativity, unconventionality.
