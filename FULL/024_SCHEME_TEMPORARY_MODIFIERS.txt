=== SECTION: REGISTRY_RULES ===

- [STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS][MODIFIER_REGISTRY] lists all MODIFIER_ID tokens.
- Each listed MODIFIER_ID must have exactly one corresponding [STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS][MOD_XX] record.
- MODIFIER_ID uses prefix MOD_.
- No duplicate MODIFIER_ID.
- Auto-removal/expiry is governance-enforced when expiry condition is met.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: INVARIANTS ===

- Temporary modifiers must not bypass baseline state governance.
- TARGET_FIELD must be canonical and valid.
- EXPIRY_CONDITION must be explicit.
- JUSTIFICATION required for creation and refresh.
- Active modifiers must not be orphaned from registry or target path.
- Magnitudes must remain bounded by realism and policy caps.
