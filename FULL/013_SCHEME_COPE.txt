=== SECTION: SCHEME_COPE ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
Immutable structural definition of coping configuration. Defines active mechanism fields, probability-bias fields, and invariants.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: ACTIVE_COPE_CONFIGURATION ===

PRIMARY_MECHANISM: ENUM [ACTIVE_COPING, PLANNING, DENIAL, SUBSTANCE_USE, VENTING]
SECONDARY_MECHANISM: ENUM [ACTIVE_COPING, PLANNING, DENIAL, SUBSTANCE_USE, VENTING]
SUBSTANCE_USE_RISK: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: COPE_PROBABILITY_BIASES ===

ACTIVE_COPING_PROBABILITY: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]
PLANNING_PROBABILITY: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]
DENIAL_PROBABILITY: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]
SUBSTANCE_USE_PROBABILITY: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]
VENTING_PROBABILITY: INTEGER_RANGE [0-100]

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: INVARIANTS ===

- PRIMARY_MECHANISM and SECONDARY_MECHANISM should differ unless justified override.
- Probability fields represent relative tendencies and do not require sum-to-100.
- SUBSTANCE_USE_RISK escalates on explicit event evidence.
