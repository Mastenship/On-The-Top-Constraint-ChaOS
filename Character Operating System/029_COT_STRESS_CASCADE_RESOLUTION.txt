=== SECTION: COT_STRESS_CASCADE_RESOLUTION ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This COT defines the cascade from stressor detection through physiological response to behavioral impulses. It translates stress cues into:
- proposed physiological state changes (proposal-only)
- temporary overlays in STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS (proposal-only)
- stress-driven impulses

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs:
- STATE_BODY (biochemical proposals)
- STATE_COPE (selection pressure proposals)
- STATE_TEMPORARY_MODIFIERS (overlay proposals)
- DECISION synthesis (impulses)
Is Informed By:
- STATE_MEMORY
- SCHEME personality modules
- STATE_RELATIONAL
- narrative input
- SCHEME_BODY and DYNAMICS_BODY
