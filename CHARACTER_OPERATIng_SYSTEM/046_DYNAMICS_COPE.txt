- SET
- TRANSITION
- ADD_MODIFIER
- REMOVE_MODIFIER
- CAP
- VETO

Forbidden operation references:
- CREATE_NEW_RECORD
- UPDATE
- INCREMENT
- DECREMENT

=== SECTION: ACUTE_SELECTION ===

ON_ACUTE_STRESS:
- Propose bounded SET/TRANSITION updates to:
  - PRIMARY_MECHANISM
  - SECONDARY_MECHANISM
  - relevant probability-bias fields

ON_SUBSTANCE_USE_EVENT:
- Propose TRANSITION or SET to increase SUBSTANCE_USE_RISK within bounds.
- If substance coping is selected, propose SET PRIMARY_MECHANISM accordingly.

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: STABILIZATION ===

ON_STABILIZATION_WITHOUT_ACTIVE_STRESS:
- Propose bounded shifts of coping probabilities toward baseline.
- Propose bounded reduction of SUBSTANCE_USE_RISK when no reinforcing cues exist.
