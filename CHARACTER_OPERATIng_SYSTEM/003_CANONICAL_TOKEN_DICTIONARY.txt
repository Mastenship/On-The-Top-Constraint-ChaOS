=== SECTION: CANONICAL_STYLE_RULES ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Canonical bracket-path tokens MUST be machine-stable.
TOKEN_STYLE:
- UPPER_SNAKE only
- No spaces
- No hyphens
- No mixed case
- IDs remain uppercase with underscores (ENT_XX, SKL_XX, ALT_XX, MEM_XX, PRD_XX, GOAL_XX, MOD_XX, PUP_XX)

CANONICAL_PATH_FORMAT:
- [MODULE_ROOT][GROUP_OR_ID][FIELD]
- Nested groups extend with additional brackets.

PROHIBITED:
- tokens containing spaces
- colon pseudo-paths
- Title Case tokens in brackets
- non-canonical roots
- legacy root translation behavior

=== SECTION_BREAK ===

=== SECTION: MODULE_ROOT_POLICY ===

[[LLM_INSTRUCTION]]: Legacy roots and alias translations are forbidden.
POLICY:
- STATE_, SCHEME_, DYNAMICS_, COGNITIVE_SCRATCHPAD, UPDATE_CASCADE_REGISTRY, LLM_FORMATTING_GUIDELINES are valid roots.
- Inputs with non-canonical roots MUST be rejected by governance with:
  INVALID_PATH_ROOT (LEGACY_ROOT_FORBIDDEN)
