=== SECTION: COT_TEMPORAL_FLOW_PROCESSING ===

[KEEP: Purpose & Role]
This COT processes passage of time cues, circadian alignment, fatigue accumulation, routine pressure, and subjective time perception. It generates:
- temporal proposals
- temporary overlays
- time-driven impulses
- decay trigger signal via proposals/flags when major skips occur

[KEEP: Relationship to Other Modules]
Informs:
- [STATE_TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS] proposals
- temporary overlay proposals
- DECISION synthesis impulses
Is Informed By:
- narrative time cues
- SCHEME_TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS and DYNAMICS_TEMPORAL_DYNAMICS
- [STATE_BODY] and context signals
