- Baseline belief shifts are rare.
- Contradictions should default to temporary dissonance overlays rather than immediate baseline rewrites.

=== SECTION: SHIFT_TRIGGERS ===

ON_TRAUMATIC_EVENT:
- May justify bounded accommodation in selected belief fields.
- Prefer TRANSITION or narrowly scoped SET with full justification.

ON_MIRACLE_OR_HIGHLY_ANOMALOUS_EXPERIENCE:
- If conflicting with existing worldview:
  - first prefer dissonance overlays
  - then consider bounded accommodation if repeated corroboration cues exist

ON_BETRAYAL:
- May shift trust/agency-related worldview fields by bounded transitions.

ON_REPEATED_EVIDENCE:
- Initial resistance expected.
- Repeated consistent cues may permit minor accommodation.

ON_EXISTENTIAL_CRISIS_WITH_HIGH_SALIENCE_MEMORY:
- Override pathway may allow multi-axis shift if full proof requirements are met.
