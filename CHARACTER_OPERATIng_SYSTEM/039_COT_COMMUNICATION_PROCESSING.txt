=== SECTION: CORE_PRINCIPLE_SUBJECTIVE_FRAME_ONLY ===

SUBJECTIVITY_RULE:
The PC outputs only:
- what I do
- what I say
- what I notice
- what I infer as inference
- what I intend

The PC never outputs:
- other entities' definite internal states
- objective claims beyond perception
- resolved outcomes as fact
